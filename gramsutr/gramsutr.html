Why GramSutr?

Urban
Rural
“Talent is universal, but opportunity is geographic. In Gopalpura, brilliance exists in every lane, but the 'Sutr' (The Connection) to the modern world is missing. GramSutr is our systematic protocol to plug urban expertise into rural grit.”

The Three Pillars

Sutr-Shiksha

Education

Focus: The "25 Gems" Project

Identifying 25 brilliant, underprivileged students for lifetime 1-to-1 mentorship, digital literacy, and scholarship support.

Goal: Bridge the gap to premier institutes (IITs / IISc /IIMs/ AIIMS)

Sutr-Swasthya

Health & Hygiene

Focus: Women's Wellness & Blood Donation

Distribution of 5,000+ hygiene kits, breaking menstrual taboos through professional workshops, and a Mega Blood Donation Camp.

Goal: 100% Sanitary Pad usage in Gopalpura

Sutr-Sashakti

Governance

Focus: Last-Mile Social Security

Setting up "Scheme Kendras" to register 1,000+ villagers for Pension, Ration, and Health insurance schemes.

Goal: No villager should be left behind due to illiteracy
